FinTech is:

  • The use of technology to improve and automate financial services.
  • A growing industry that includes everything from mobile banking apps to cryptocurrency platforms
  • A way to make financial processes faster, more accessible, and more efficient for both businesses and consumers

This semester, I’m interested in learning more about the key technologies that are shaping the future of FinTech. I want to explore general FinTech concepts and how they apply to real-world financial services. I’m especially curious about AWS infrastructure, artificial intelligence, blockchain, and how these tools are used to build smarter, more secure financial applications. Understanding how all these pieces work together will help me grow in my career and stay current in a fast-changing industry.
